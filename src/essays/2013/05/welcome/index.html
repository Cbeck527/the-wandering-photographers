Welcome!

May 22, 2013

There I was, sitting in the back of Emily's car on our way back from an awesome day taking pictures at the park, when it dawned on me. College is over. Mind you, graduation had already come and gone, but it still didn't really sink in. The realization only happened in the aftershock: the great Syracuse exodus had begun.

With everyone moving to different parts of the country, I wanted a way for us to collectively document our experiences. 

A few google searches and some code later, The Wandering Photographers was born. 

Enjoy.

// Chris
