Creative Recharge

June 8, 2013

For the second consecrative year I have gone to Canada with my dad and his friends. While the fishing and catching up with friends is the most important part, this trip allows me to recharge creatively. The landscape is beautiful, the sunsets are beautiful, the sky is beautiful. Did I mention it was beautiful?

Finding a way to creatively recharge is always important for creative people. While finding that spot may be challenging, the payoff in the long run is well worth it.
